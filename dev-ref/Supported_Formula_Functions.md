## SUMPRODUCT

` since 3.7.0`

You can specify a condition for an array formula to just calculate
partial cells in a given range. For example,

`=SUMPRODUCT(--(A1:A3="John"),(B1:B3),(C1:C3))`

or simpler

`=SUMPRODUCT((F12:F21=1)*(G12:G21="Z")*H12:H21)`
